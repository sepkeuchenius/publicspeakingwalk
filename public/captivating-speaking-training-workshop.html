Captivating Speaking Training Workshop Captivating Speaking Training Workshop

This workshop will boost the speaking confidence of you and your team.

In the 3,5h workshop all elements of what makes a great speech are discussed. The elements are brought to life through demonstrations, and through rounds of deliberate practice from all participants (4-10). You will get tangible advice, and an immediate opportunity to put these lessons into practice. The practice rounds are followed with honest, yet considerate feedback. The practice creates more self awareness, which leads to more effective practice again. This workshop will boost the speaking confidence of you and your team.
