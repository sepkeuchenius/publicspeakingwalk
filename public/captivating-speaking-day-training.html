Day Training Day Training

The Day Training has the potential to transform the way you present yourself when public speaking. In the 6h workshop all elements of what makes a great speech are discussed. The elements are brought to life through demonstrations, and through even more rounds of deliberate practice from all participants (4-10). You will get tangible advice, and an immediate opportunity to put these lessons into practice. The practice rounds are followed with honest, yet considerate feedback. In the Day Training we will go through the practice - feedback cycle for enough times to make visible, hearable and palpable improvement. Why does it help to become a better speaker?

It’s a skill that can determine a big part of your success in every aspect of your life. Yes, it makes your ideas be heard, it makes you more respected and liked more whenever you’re asked to give a presentation at your job. However, It also trickles down into your everyday life. Public speaking is speaking in public. When are you speaking in public? To a degree, almost all the time. Speaking training is an effective method to become more aware of how others perceive you, so you can choose to optimize for confidence, kindness and connection.
